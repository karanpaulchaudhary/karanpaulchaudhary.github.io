Questions

  • What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important.

    • The site which works on every device ,no matter what is the screen resolution is known as responsive site.It is very important because ,lets say you booked your plane ticket on desktop and that page does not open on your mobile ,this can create a problem.

  • What mobile first design is, and why it's important.

    • 60% of your website users use mobile to open the website,so it becomes important to test your site on mobile first and then on desktop.

  • What frameworks are, and their pros and cons.
